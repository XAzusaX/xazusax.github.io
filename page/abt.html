About Me.

“I don’t build websites. I build spaces where thoughts can stay a little longer.”

Azusa

University Student • Developer • Creator

Reverie Archive started as a personal experiment while I was studying. It wasn’t planned as a portfolio at first — more like a space to try things out and see what I could build on my own. Over time, it became a place where I organize ideas, UI experiments, and small systems that don’t always belong to a finished project. I usually work step by step, adjusting as I go instead of following a fixed plan. Even now, I’m still refining my direction — not just technically, but also in terms of the kind of work I want to create in the future. This project is still evolving along with me.
